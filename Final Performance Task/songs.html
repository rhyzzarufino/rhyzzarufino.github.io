Album:"THE FIRST STEP: CHAPTER ONE"

Writing: TREASURE’s Haruto and Choi Hyun-Suk in collaboration with Se.A and Choice37 Release: 7th August, 2020 “Boy” is a fun and colorful song about a boy’s desire for a girl. The lyrics speak of his admiration for her and how he wishes to have her as his girlfriend. Passion and obsession are alluded to through striking visuals.
